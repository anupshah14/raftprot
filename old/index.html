About

Lipid rafts are specialized membrane microdomains enriched in cholesterol and sphingolipids. They are regarded as dynamic signalling and sorting platforms that regulate a diverse range of cellular functions. Lipid raft dysfunction has been implicated in a range of diseases. RaftProt is a comprehensive resource for membrane lipid-raft associated proteins reported in proteomics studies. It provides multiple search functions to facilitate knowledge-based interrogation of existing data.
