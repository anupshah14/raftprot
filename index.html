Lipid rafts are cholesterol-rich membrane microdomains that regulate a diverse range of cellular functions. RaftProt is a searchable database of proteins published in mammalian lipid raft proteomics studies. A subset of ‘High Confidence’ bona fide lipid raft proteins was derived based on the criteria of reported identification by more than one biochemical isolation method and/or sensitivity to extraction by the cholesterol-depleting agent methyl-beta-cyclodextrin.

Please cite us when you publish results using the database:

Shah A, Chen D, Boda AR, Foster LJ, Davis MJ, Hill MM (2015) RaftProt:mammalian lipid raft proteome database. Nucleic Acids Res. 43, D335-338
